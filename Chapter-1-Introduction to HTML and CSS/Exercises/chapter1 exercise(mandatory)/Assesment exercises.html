Samosa

Samosa is one of famous snack in India and countries in Asia and Africa .It is a fried South Asian pastry with a savoury
filling and accompanied by chutney. Most of the time is filled with potato but it can non vegetarian or vegetarian according to filling. The tasty is marvellous.
The form of samosa comes in triangular, cone, or half-moon shapes, depending on the region. This pastry have originated from medieval times.

    Method

    To make dough:

    • Add 1 cup all purpose flour in a bowl
    • Add 1/2 ajwain carom seeds
    • Add 1/2 salt or according to taste
    • mix it together then add oil
    • Finally mix all together,add add water step by step while knead the dough into stiff dough, don't overwork the dough nor knead the dough.
      It must come together and form a stiff dough.
    • Cover the dough with a moist cloth and let it rest for 40 minutes.
      Now let go make the filling.

    .............................................................................................................

    Filling

    1. peel the skin of the potato then boil or
      boil the potato with peel on as you like
    2. Boil potatoes until soft
    3. Mash the soft potatos.
      set aside
    4. Heat a pan
    5. Pour 1 tablespoon of oil
    6. Onces the oil is hot,add cumin seeds
    7. Let the seeds cool for few seconds
    8. Add chopped ginger,green chili and onion
    9. Add the spices and salt
    10. Cook it turns to golden brown
    11. Add Lentil
    12. Add mashed potatoes
    13. mix all together
    14. check if salt and spices are enough enough,if not add little
    15. mix them together,Once everything is combined.
      remove the pan from heat and off the heat

    Lets make Samosa
    1. Take the dough and give quick knead,then divide into 4 equal dough.
    2. begin working on first dough
    3. roll the dough in circle oval shape
    4. if the dough very big and
      cut equal circle with circle cutter or plate and knife
    5. spread little warter in one part
    6. bring the two ends of the straight edge together to form a cone
    7. pinch the pointed ends to make a perfect cone
    8. fill the samosa with filling.Do not over fill it.
    9. spread the water all around the circumference of the cone since you have to seal it.
      Pinch the opposite side.
    10. now pinch the edges and seal the samosa.
      now the first samosa is ready

      11. now do this same step with the remaining dough

    Fry the samosa
    1. heat oil in an frying pan
    2. Check if the oil is hot by putting a small piece of dough.
      When it comes to the surface, it shows that the oil is ready.
    3. Drop the samosa gently into the oil.
    4. fry it at low flame.
    5. Around 10 to 15 minutes.The samosa will be firm and light brown color
    6. Now increase the flame to medium and fry until it gets golden brown
    7. After it is brown get it out of the fry pan to the plate or Stainless Steel Wire Skimmer Spoon
    8. Fry the rest of the samosa in the same manner
    Now you have delicious samosa
