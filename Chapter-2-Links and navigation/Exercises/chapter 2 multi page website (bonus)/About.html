lolirock

lolirock is a music,action,comedy,adventure,fantasy animation series. The series begins with the two teenage
girls searching for the protagonist on the Earth. She is the princess of their world and they want
her to help them to defeat the sorcerer Gramorr the main villain To locate the protaganist,
they organize audition for singing.However, the protagonist is unware that she is a princess of Ephedia,
which also their home planet.Later,the protagonist learns about her life and trains her powers
with the help of two teens in secret on earth.She join them to fight the villains.
They form a rock band and have fun while living on earth.
In season 2 they defeat the Gromorr and one of his assissant.One assissant returns to Ephedia for revenge.
They then go back to earth to perform, hiding her powers. Lolirock is a french animated
television series produced by Banijay Kids & Family with the participation of French Television
and the Walt Disney company France.The series was created on october 18,2014 in France by
program creators David Michel and Jean Louis Vandestoc and composer Nobert Gilbert and
written by Madellaine Paxson.First published in France on 18th october 2014 on France 3,and
expanded to televisions channels in Europe
