lolirock

lolirock is a music,action,comedy,adventure,fantasy animation series. The series begins with the two teenage
girls searching for the protagonist on the Earth. She is the princess of their world and they want
her to help them to defeat the sorcerer Gramorr the main villain To locate the protaganist,
they organize audition for singing.However, the protagonist is unware that she is a princess of Ephedia,
which also their home planet.Later,the protagonist learns about her life and trains her powers
with the help of two teens in secret on earth.She join them to fight the villains.
They form a rock band and have fun while living on earth.
In season 2 they defeat the Gromorr and one of his assissant and returns to Ephedia.
They then go back to earth to perform, hiding her powers.

The series was created on october 18,2014 in France by program creators David Michel and
Jean Louis Vandestoc and composer Nobert Gilbert and written by Madellaine Paxson.

Plot

Planet Ephedia is controlled by sorcerer Gramorr before the attack it was controlled by the Queen and king of Ephedia.
When the attack took place, the queen sent her infant daughter(the protagonist) with Lady Ellira, an Ephedian warrior and
bodyguard of the king and queen to the earth, Since she was the last hope to save their home. The queen asks her to raise
her daughter and not reveal herself as Ephedra. But Grommor could not take the throne since the throne and the crown was protected
by a magical power and the oracle Gems of the crown got scattered on the earth. To find all he sends his minions to the earth. On the
other side the two teenage girls Talia and Auriana from the Ephedia planet to Earth in search of the protagonist to join the girls to defeat
Grommor.They organize a singing audition to locate the girl. After a couple of auditions, they find Iris take her to a place and show her past.
She learns about her past and begins to uncover her powers step by step. The girls train and work together to defeat the
